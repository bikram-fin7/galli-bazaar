What this isn't: a broker, an exchange, or financial advice — it has no affiliation with NEPSE, SEBON, MeroShare, or any licensed brokerage. Market data, including the composite index shown above, is pulled on a best-effort basis from unofficial, community-maintained tools (credit: the open-source NepseAPI-Unofficial project) and automatically falls back to a synchronized simulation when that feed is unavailable — never use prices shown here to inform a real trade. Coverage: when the live feed is reachable, the Markets tab shows NEPSE's real, full list of traded equities; when it isn't, trading falls back to a smaller curated list of real, verified companies across every NEPSE sector. Either way, only ordinary equity shares are included — debentures, government bonds, mutual fund units, and promoter shares are intentionally left out, since those aren't traded here the way ordinary company stock is.
